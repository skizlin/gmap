## Notes for mapping logic

- **Bet365:** `main.sp.*` blocks have `id` (e.g. 910000, 910216); odds rows use `name` (Winner / Handicap / Total), `header` (1/2), `handicap` (line or `O 184.5`), `odds`.
- **Bwin:** Each row is often one `Markets[]` entry per line (`templateId` + `attr`); outcomes in `results`.
- **1xbet:** `Value.GE[]` — **`G`** is the market id; **`E`** is rows of outcome cells; **`P`** = line, **`T`** = outcome type, **`C`** = decimal odds.
