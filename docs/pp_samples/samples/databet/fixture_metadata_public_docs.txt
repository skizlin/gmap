DATA.BET trading feed — fixture geography / demographics (public integration doc)
==================================================================================

What appears in published snapshot examples (feed integration v2):
- fixture.tournament — id + name only. Competition context (including league vs cup)
  is carried here; naming may encode youth tiers (e.g. "U21 ..." in the official doc example).
- fixture.venue — at least country_code (e.g. GB, BR) when present.

What we did NOT find in the public snapshot/fixture JSON examples:
- Dedicated fields named gender, age_group, amateur/professional, or ISO region codes
  separate from venue/tournament naming.

Practical implication: if you need structured gender or age classification, confirm with
your DATA.BET integration contact whether your product tier adds extension fields,
metadata APIs, or whether you derive tier from tournament naming and sport rules.
